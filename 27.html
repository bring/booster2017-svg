Workshop exercise:
Create a landscape

Your task is to play around with drawing and writing SVG. Use this HTML&CSS as a starter kit. Delete the <polygon> and <path> to add your own mountains, oceans, or perhaps a city skyline? Go for any theme you feel like, or pick from the suggestions. Write something here to complement the scenery — or just delete the text. But don’t forget to take Victor Vector on an adventure with you!
